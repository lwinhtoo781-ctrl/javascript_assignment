1. col-sm-6 Applies to screens 576px and above. The column will take half width (6/12) on small devices and larger. On screens below 576px (extra small phones), it becomes full width. 2. col-md-6 Applies to screens 768px and above. The column becomes half width on medium screens (tablets) and larger. On smaller screens (<768px), the column is full width. 3. col-lg-6 Applies to screens 992px and above. The column becomes half width only on large screens (laptops/desktops). On medium and small screens (<992px), it stays full width.
